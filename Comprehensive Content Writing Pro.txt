# Comprehensive Content Writing Prompt for Metanova Tech Website

## Company Context
Metanova Tech is a B2B growth collective founded in 2020, specializing in helping technology companies succeed through data operations, demand strategy, lead generation, and creative media services. Target clients include SaaS companies, IT services, staffing agencies, consultants, marketing agencies, manufacturing firms, and logistics providers.

## Content Requirements for Cursor AI

Create comprehensive, SEO-optimized website content for Metanova Tech (https://metanovatech.com/) with the following specifications:

### General Guidelines:
- **Tone**: Professional, confident, results-driven, yet approachable
- **Style**: Clear, engaging B2B marketing language that emphasizes value and outcomes
- **SEO Focus**: Naturally incorporate relevant keywords without keyword stuffing
- **Length**: Each page should be 800-1500 words minimum for optimal SEO
- **Structure**: Use H1, H2, H3 headings strategically; include bullet points and short paragraphs for readability
- **CTA Integration**: Include compelling calls-to-action throughout each page

### Primary Keywords to Target:
- B2B lead generation
- B2B demand generation

## Additional Content Elements to Include Across All Pages:

1. **Internal Linking Strategy**: Link between related service pages and to relevant blog posts

3. **Meta Descriptions**: Compelling 150-160 character descriptions for each page
4. **FAQ Sections**: Include 5-7 relevant FAQs per service page for SEO
5. **Related Resources**: Suggest related content, case studies, or guides
6. **Trust Signals**: Industry certifications, partnerships, client logos (placeholder sections)
7. **CTA Variations**: Multiple CTA options (consultation, audit, guide download, etc.)

## SEO Optimization Instructions:

- Target 1-2 primary keywords per page, 3-5 secondary keywords
- Keyword density: 1-2% (natural integration)
- Include long-tail keywords in H2 and H3 headings
- Use semantic keywords and related terms throughout
- Optimize for featured snippets with clear, concise answers
- Include statistics and data points where relevant
- Write for humans first, search engines second
- Use active voice and power words
- Keep paragraphs under 4 lines for readability
- Include transition words for flow

---

## Voice and Messaging Guidelines:

**DO:**
- Emphasize outcomes and results
- Use confident, authoritative language
- Show expertise without being arrogant
- Include specific examples and methodologies
- Address pain points directly
- Demonstrate understanding of B2B tech challenges
- Use industry terminology appropriately
- Create urgency without being pushy

**DON'T:**
- Use generic marketing jargon excessively
- Make unrealistic promises
- Compare negatively to competitors
- Use overly technical language that alienates
- Create walls of text without breaks
- Forget mobile readers
- Neglect the human element
